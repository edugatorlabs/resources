# Edugator Experience Survey

## For Instructors

As an instructor you can either ask your students to fill out this survey (https://forms.gle/w4my7wNweBpgAUuE8) and request the anonymous data by emailing kapooramanpreet@ufl.edu **or** create your own survey with the following information:

---

## Survey Overview

This survey consists of questions about your experiences using the Edugator platform. Your feedback is valuable and will help us improve the platform to better meet your educational needs. Please answer each question honestly and thoughtfully. 

**Estimated time:** 5-10 minutes  
**Confidentiality:** All responses are anonymous and will be used solely for research purposes.
